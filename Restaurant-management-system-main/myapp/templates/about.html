About Us

Based in Chicago, Illinois, The Ratoutille is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

The Ratoutille is owned by two Egyptian brothers, Ahmed and Mohamed, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Ahmed relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy.

Mohamed does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
